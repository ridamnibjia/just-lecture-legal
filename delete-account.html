Delete My Account

Permanently remove your account and all associated personal data from Just Lecture.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, your profile, course preferences, and usage history are removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your name, email address, and profile picture
  • Your phone number
  • Your course and category selection
  • Your watch history and last-played video position
  • Your authentication tokens (access & refresh)
  • All account data held on our backend servers

Data stored locally on your device (encrypted session cache) is automatically cleared when you log out. You can log out first from the app's Profile screen before submitting this request.

How to Request Deletion

Since Just Lecture uses Google Sign-In, account deletion is handled by emailing us directly. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation.

  1. Tap the button below to open a pre-filled deletion request email in your mail app. Make sure you send it from the same email address linked to your Just Lecture account.
  2. Confirm your identity — include your registered email and name in the email so we can locate your account quickly.
  3. Send the email. We will process the request within 30 days and reply to confirm deletion.
Send Deletion Request →

Or email us manually at justlectureapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

What to Include in Your Email

Copy the template below if you prefer to write your own email:

To: justlectureapp@gmail.com Subject: Account Deletion Request – Just Lecture Hi Just Lecture Team, I would like to request permanent deletion of my account and all associated personal data from Just Lecture. Name: [Your full name] Registered email: [Email linked to your Just Lecture account] Please confirm once the deletion is complete. Thank you.

What Happens Next

Timeline Action
Within 3 days We acknowledge your request by email
Within 30 days Your account and all personal data are permanently deleted from our servers
After deletion You receive a confirmation email; your data cannot be recovered after this point

If you change your mind, you can create a new account at any time using Google Sign-In — though previous data cannot be restored after deletion.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process, contact us at justlectureapp@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 3 business days.
